WEG* font is a —totally— experimental typography where legibility isn’t the focus.

During the last year I was studing how the signs are constructed and how can I modify or reconstruct these rules. I wanted to play and I didn't pay attention to the legibility. If you're looking for a font to use in a project where to readibility is importat, I suggest you to pick Telder ht, Alegreya ht, Andada ht, Cira ht, Piazzolla or any another type from our catalogue.


There is a single line that builds all the paragraphs and pages.
This typeface contains uppercase, lining figures and basic punctuation in three weigh, regular, liight and thin. A medida que decrece el peso crece la legibilidad de los signos.
This is the fist stages of the experiment. It’s a project in expansion. I have developed Spanish, Guaraní and English scripts. If you need any other language, please let me know. I would like to expand the character set.
